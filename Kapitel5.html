Survival Guide

In relation to your free time, someone may ask:

Was machst du gern in deiner Freizeit? = What do you like to do in yout free time?

Ich (verb) gern = I like to (verb)

If you want to be more specific with your verb. So, for example
I like to read:

Ich lese gern.

But what if I want to say 'I like to read romance novels'?

You put the second, non-verb part, so the 'romance novels', after 'gern'.

Ich lese gern romantische Romane.

Hast du ein Lieblingshobby? = Do you have a favourite hobby.

There are other ways of saying you may prefer or like/favourite something:

lieber = prefer

am liebsten

-placed at the end of your verb

Fernsehen is something called a trendbar verb. It is a verb that is comprised of two parts

In this case a word 'fern' and the ver 'sehen'.

When using a trendbar verb, the 'verb' part stays in the second position whilst the first part goes to the end.

Eg. Fernsehen: Ich sehe gern fern.
